## addy.io has partnered with EasyOptOuts

We're excited to share that addy.io has partnered with [EasyOptOuts](https://www.easyoptouts.com/), an automated data removal service that scrubs your name, address, and phone number from 200+ data brokers and people-search sites.

addy.io Lite and Pro subscribers can now get 25% off for life. Each coupon covers 5 sign-ups, so you can protect yourself and the people you care about. **US customers only**.

How to claim your discount

<ol>
    <li>1. Log in to your addy.io dashboard</li>
    <li>2. Click your username (top-right) → “addy.io Perks”</li>
    <li>3. Find the EasyOptOuts offer and click “Redeem code”</li>
    <li>4. Use the code at easyoptouts.com when subscribing</li>
</ol>

Read EasyOptOuts's announcement [here](https://www.easyoptouts.com/announcements).
