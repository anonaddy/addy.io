## addy.io has partnered with Kagi

We’re excited to share that addy.io has partnered with [Kagi](https://kagi.com/), a premium, privacy-focused, ad-free search engine that prioritises user experience over advertising revenue.

addy.io Lite and Pro subscribers can now get 3 months of Kagi's Professional plan for free (unlimited search).

How to claim your discount

<ol>
    <li>1. Log in to your addy.io dashboard</li>
    <li>2. Click your username (top-right) → “addy.io Perks”</li>
    <li>3. Find the Kagi offer and click “Redeem code”</li>
    <li>4. Click on the link "Click to visit Kagi and apply code"</li>
</ol>

## The new Perks page

The perks page can be found at [https://addy.io/perks/](/perks/). New partnerships will be added over time.
