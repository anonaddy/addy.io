## addy.io has partnered with Windscribe

We’re excited to share that addy.io has partnered with [Windscribe](https://windscribe.com/), the Court-proven, audited no-logs VPN service with post-quantum safe encryption.

addy.io Lite and Pro subscribers can now get a 3 month Pro trial (no strings attached), then the annual Pro plan for 49 USD per year (regular price 69 USD).

How to claim your discount

<ol>
    <li>1. Log in to your addy.io dashboard</li>
    <li>2. Click your username (top-right) → “addy.io Perks”</li>
    <li>3. Find the Windscribe offer and click “Redeem code”</li>
    <li>4. Use the code at windscribe.com when subscribing</li>
</ol>

Read Windscribes's announcement [here](https://windscribe.com/blog/windscribe-partnerships/).
