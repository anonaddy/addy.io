## addy.io has partnered with Tuta

We're excited to announce our new partnership with [Tuta](https://tuta.com/), the privacy-focused email service that provides end-to-end encrypted email, calendars, and contacts with zero access to your data (no scanning, no ads, no tracking). Tuta uses strong post-quantum encryption and is a true advocate for user privacy, making it easy for anyone to protect their communications from surveillance.

This partnership means you can combine addy.io's unlimited email aliases (to hide your real inbox from spam, trackers, and breaches) with Tuta's secure mailboxes, forwarding aliases straight to your encrypted Tuta account for double privacy protection.

addy.io Lite and Pro subscribers can now get 25% off your first year.
